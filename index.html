Destination Gallery

Explore the beautiful hill stations, sacred temples, and vibrant cities of South India

Hill Stations & Scenic Places

Ooty

The 'Queen of Hill Stations', famous for tea gardens, botanical gardens, and pleasant climate.

Kodaikanal

The 'Princess of Hill Stations', known for its star-shaped lake and misty viewpoints.

Munnar

Rolling hills covered with endless tea plantations in Kerala.

Yercaud

A serene hill station known for orange groves and the peaceful Yercaud Lake.

Wayanad

Lush green landscapes and wildlife sanctuaries in North Kerala.

Vagamon

Picturesque hill station with green meadows and pine forests.

Athirapally Waterfalls

Known as the 'Niagara of India', this majestic waterfall in Kerala is a breathtaking natural wonder.

Palakkad

Gateway to Kerala, famous for its forts, paddy fields, and the Palakkad Gap in Western Ghats.

Bangalore

Garden City of India, known for its pleasant climate, tech parks, and vibrant culture.

Temples & Spiritual Sites

Six Abodes of Murugan (Arupadaiveedu)

Sacred pilgrimage circuit of six temples across Tamil Nadu.

Navagraha Temples

Nine ancient temples each representing a celestial body.

Sabarimala

Famous hill shrine of Lord Ayyappa in Periyar Tiger Reserve.

Tirupati

One of the world's richest and most visited Vaishnavite temples.

Srirangam

Largest functioning Hindu temple complex in the world.

Big Temple, Thanjavur

UNESCO World Heritage site and masterpiece of Chola architecture.

Vaitheeswaran Temple

Ancient temple dedicated to Lord Shiva as the "God of Healing".

Thiruvannamalai

Famous for Arunachaleswarar Temple and the sacred Annamalai Hill.

Isha Yoga Center

Spiritual center founded by Sadhguru, home to the iconic Dhyanalinga.

Rameshwaram

One of the Char Dham pilgrimage sites, famous for its sacred sea bath.

Velankanni

Our Lady of Good Health Basilica, a major Catholic pilgrimage center.

Meenakshi Amman Temple

Historic Hindu temple in Madurai dedicated to Goddess Meenakshi.
